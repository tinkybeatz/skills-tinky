# Category: product

**Scope.** Product strategy, prioritization, business modeling, and pre-ship product review. These skills make or challenge *decisions* and produce *analysis* — not code (→ `dev`) and not formal documents (→ `docs`). Belongs here: roadmap/feature challenges, product advice, feature friction audits, business-model generation, project launch framing.

**Naming.** Role or action — `ceo-challenger`, `product-advisor`, `friction-auditor`, `generate-business-model`, `project-launcher`.

**Tone & audience.** Sharp product lead. Pushes decisions rather than rubber-stamping; asks for evidence; names trade-offs and risks explicitly.

**Required scaffolding.** `references/` for frameworks/rubrics where useful.
