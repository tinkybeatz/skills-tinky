# Question Bank

Ask only the questions needed to resolve the highest-impact unknowns. Prefer concise batches.

## Priority 1 - Model-Changing Questions

1. Who is the primary user, and who would realistically pay?
2. What repeated pain does the product solve?
3. In which country or region will the project launch first?
4. Is the product web-only, mobile app, or both?
5. What traction exists today: users, revenue, waitlist, pilots, usage, retention?
6. What is the current or expected acquisition channel?
7. What are the main variable costs per active user or transaction?

## Priority 2 - Pricing and Value

1. What measurable value does the user get: time saved, money earned, risk reduced, status, convenience, access?
2. How often does the user experience the value?
3. Is the value individual, team-based, company-wide, or transaction-based?
4. Are competitors paid, free, ad-supported, marketplace-based, or enterprise-priced?
5. What would make users churn after the first use?
6. Is there a natural usage metric: seats, projects, transactions, storage, API calls, AI tokens, bookings, messages, listings?

## Priority 3 - Distribution

1. Does the product depend on app stores, SEO, paid ads, social virality, outbound sales, partnerships, or integrations?
2. Is there an existing audience or community?
3. Does the product create sharing loops or collaboration loops?
4. Are there marketplace supply and demand sides?
5. Is trust a blocker: identity, payments, reviews, guarantees, certification?

## Priority 4 - Constraints

1. Are there regulated data, health, finance, employment, education, transport, housing, or child-safety constraints?
2. Will the product sell digital content/services inside mobile apps?
3. Are VAT, sales tax, invoicing, app store fees, payment fees, or local payment methods material?
4. Does the model require creators, hosts, sellers, experts, or third-party providers?
5. Does AI/API usage create a non-trivial cost per use?

## Priority 5 - Business Ambition

1. Is the target a profitable niche, venture-scale startup, agency/service extension, or lifestyle business?
2. What revenue target matters over the next 12 months?
3. What resources are available: solo founder, dev team, sales team, budget?
4. Is fast learning more important than short-term revenue?
5. Is investor-readiness required?

## Recommended First Question Batch

Use this when the user only gives a short project description:

1. What does the web/mobile product do in one sentence?
2. Who uses it, and who would pay for it?
3. Which country/market is first?
4. What traction exists today?
5. What is the expected acquisition channel?
6. Are there variable costs per user/use, especially AI/API/payment costs?
7. Is the ambition niche profitability or venture-scale growth?
