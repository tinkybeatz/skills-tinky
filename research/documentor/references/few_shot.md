# Deep format example — output calibration

This example shows **the structure and discipline expected** of a DOCUMENTOR Deep
delivery. A compact mini-example (~ 700 tokens) to anchor the format.
Reproduce this structure and this sourcing discipline, adapting the depth to the
actual topic — a real Deep deliverable is 3 to 8 times this size.

## Executive summary

[5–10 dense lines: temporal framing + version if tech, main recommendation,
overall confidence level, signal on the gray areas. No pointless preamble,
no fluff. The reader must be able to stop here and have 80% of the value.]

## Non-negotiable discipline

- Every factual claim `[F]` is followed **inline** by a citation `[Title — URL — date]` — never only in a block at the end.
- **At least 1 Tier 1 source** (primary) on the topic, otherwise refuse the deliverable.
- Mandatory labels: `[F]` facts, `[R]` derived recommendations, `[H]` hypotheses.
- Mandatory sections: Executive summary → Key points → Ranked sources → Contradictions → Confidence → Next steps.
