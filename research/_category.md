# Category: research

**Scope.** Gather, understand, explain, and ingest external information. Belongs here: multi-source web research, research briefs, explaining/teaching a concept, and analyzing media (e.g. video → structured report). The emphasis is on *understanding and synthesis*; if the goal is a formal client deliverable document, that leans `docs`.

**Naming.** Descriptive of the activity — `documentor`, `research-brief`, `vulgarize`, `youtube-analyzer`.

**Tone & audience.** Rigorous and cited. Distinguishes evidence from inference; surfaces sources; flags uncertainty.

**Required scaffolding.** `references/` for methodology and source-scoring rubrics; `scripts/` for fetch/transcription helpers.

**Default frontmatter.**
```yaml
name: <folder-name>
description: <what it does + trigger phrases>
user-invokable: true
```

**Skills here.** documentor · research-brief · vulgarize · youtube-analyzer
